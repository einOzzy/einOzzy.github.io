Eligibility & Requirements

Do you have what it takes to join the roster?

Skill & Experience

We hold our team to high standards. Your skills and experience will be thoughtfully reviewed, with the final decision made by your chosen department Lead.

Commitment

Members are expected to remain consistently active within the team and community.

Age & Region

Minimum 14 years of age, and all regions are welcome. Just be aware that high ping or big time zone differences could limit your ability to play in live community events like for ex. freestyle tournaments.

Communication

Clear English communication, high-quality microphone, and active Discord presence.

Hardware

Muste be able to upload clips in at least 1080p 60 fps quality.

Professionalism

Zero tolerance for toxicity. Players must exhibit discipline and respect for the unit.

What We Evaluate

A deep dive into the criteria that define a Lucent player.

Creativity

We value individuals who think beyond the ordinary and bring fresh ideas to everything they do. Whether it's unique mechanics, graphics, innovative content concepts, or a distinct personal style, we look for people who aren't afraid to stand out and push creative boundaries.

Originality

We look for individuals who bring something unique to the team. Whether through their playstyle, content, editing, or personal brand, we value those who develop their own identity rather than following trends and strive to leave a lasting impression.

Activity

Members are expected to remain consistently active within the team and community. Regular participation, communication, and contribution are required to maintain a strong and engaged environment.

Receptivity

Members should be open to feedback, critique, guidance, and new ideas. A willingness to learn, adapt, and improve is essential for growth within the team.

Consistency

Members are expected to consistently maintain the quality of their work and contributions. Reliable performance, steady improvement, and regular involvement are essential.

Dedication

We value individuals who are committed to continuous growth and improvement. Whether through practicing their craft, creating content, or supporting the team, we look for people who consistently put in the effort and remain focused on achieving long-term goals.

What departments can I apply for?

You can apply for Freestyler, Competitive Clip Hitter, Editor, Designer, No Flipper, or Staff and Management positions. Just select your preferred department on the application form.

What do you look for in Designer and Editor applicants?

We look for highly skilled creators who can deliver clean, professional work. For Designers, this means high-quality team assets and profile pictures. For Editors, we look for excellent editing, effects, and a clean, professional style.

Do I need a high competitive rank to join as a player?

For most of our roster-such as Freestylers and No Flippers-we prioritize mechanical skill, original style, and clip quality over your standard matchmaking rank. However, the Competitive Clip Hitter role does have a strict ranked requirement: applicants must be Grand Champion 3 (GC3) or higher.

Can I apply for Staff or Management if I am not a player or creator?

Yes, absolutely! We are always looking for reliable staff members to help moderate our community and professional management to help run the team behind the scenes.

How are applications reviewed?

Every application, portfolio, or clip submission is sent directly to the Lead of your chosen department. They will personally evaluate your work to see if it matches our high standards.
