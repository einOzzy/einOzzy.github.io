WHO WE ARE

Founded April 28, 2026

Team Lucent is a high standard competitive Rocket League team built on structure, discipline, and performance. We focus on improving as a unit and competing at a high level through consistency and strong teamwork.

Our goal is simple: develop skilled players, refine our gameplay, and establish ourselves as a respected name in the Rocket League scene.

Lucent is built for players who are committed to improvement and serious about competition.


OUR MISSION

Grow

We aim to continuously expand and strengthen our community by developing talent and welcoming dedicated creators. We strive to create more opportunities for members to succeed and make an impact.

Refine

Continuously improving the quality of our work by developing skills, creativity, and attention to detail. We strive to push our standards higher and deliver the best content possible.

Establish

Our goal is to establish ourselves as a respected name in the Rocket League scene, known for our professional conduct and relentless pursuit of excellence.
